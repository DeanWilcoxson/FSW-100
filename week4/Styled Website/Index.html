My Goals

My goals are to provide a better lifestyle for my kids than I had as a child. Also, to own a piece of property with a nice view my family and I can live on. I will accomplish and acquire these things through sheer determination and persistence. Practice makes perfect as they say, so even if I fail at first I wont give up and I'll continue learning all the skills I'll need. Navigating through the unknown future is scary, thats why I believe its best to develop a plan of action to guide you through the monotony of everyday life. This includes a daily routine to help you deliberate the important things you want to accomplish in simple small steps spread out over months or years. Time passes whether you are bettering yourself or not, with a routine you can grab your future before it slips out of your hands.
