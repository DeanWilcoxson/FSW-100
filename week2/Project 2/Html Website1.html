Being Indomitable
  1. Goku, the main charachter of Dragon ball z, has an indomitable spirit and does the right thing in every situation.
  2. Dark souls is a game that has raised a lot of attention for its impossible to beat game play, you really have to have an indomitable willpower to beat the game.
  3. Eminem's music is aimed toward people in tough spots in life and generally has an encouraging tone although it may not seem like it on the surface.
Reasons to strive to be indomitable
  • Challenging yourself is the only way to overcome adversity.
  • Looking at a challenge as beneficial will help change your mindset from fixed to flexible.
  • Being a better example for the eyes watching whether its your children, siblings or friends, if you don't flinch when troubles arise you will be more respected and help instill that in them.
